What's Around?

This is a blog! (I am trying to be, and infact am humourous xD)

This isn't for those who prefer just prowling across the internet, pretending to find interesting stuffs to do, though you are sure you won't be doing, but still! This is a place for people in search of wisdom, that never asked you to stay, but still you never wish to leave. A kind of wisdom hustled from the variegated flower that blooms as feelings in one's garden of impulse, that beats eggs and makes sandwiches. Here we stack up those flowers from the people who will their forbidden ones to the world. Stay here, Share your flowers, Espy on their ripening, and Taste their luscious flavour, dear dwellers! And to the ripeners, infact your being paid, you don't have to pay even heed, just read, and find your way, else make up a symbiosis and go dwelling!

Urgh... Why am I such a gasbag? Why would a flower beat pulses and eggs, and make sandwiches? What kinda shit is this? Hey people, it's nothing of that sort. Just find around really some random stuff, maybe some from the heart, deep while some just lame. Let's not dump them into a small pit. But let them take a perilous journey through the tunnels, that unite as the words get naked, bringing out it's essence. You might find a lots of Why's and How's in this blog, for which I found the answers nowhere. I'll be to indebted if any were to solve those unaccomplished quests. Read and take pleasure. Share yours and get them featured here. This is gonna be real fun. And eat more impulsed sandwiches.
