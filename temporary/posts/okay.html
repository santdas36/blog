It's okay!


It's ok that the thing isn't over.
It's ok that you had a fall.
It's ok that you're frightened
and that you'll get through'em all.
It's ok that you climb too hard,
to find yourself in some pitfall.
And that, you now know it isn't over,
It's nothing-less-than-okay to try, y'all.
It's ok that you think you were never born,
or you wish everyone else be dead and gone.
It's ok that the clock is ticking
and you're running out of choice.
Somewhere between chaos and doritos,
you'll find your voice or you'll be played their toys.
It's ok that its 3 in the morning and freezing cold outside
and you're still awake, trying to comfort yourself
with the warmth of lies that were spun by your wise.
And that there awaits after dark, an alluring sunrise,
It's ok that it's three and you're still awake.
It's ok that you're unable to pick out
what is reality and that the fantasy
unless and until you enjoy your pies.
It's ok that the things have changed
and now that ctrl+z won't work,
you'll have to learn to lurk.
Its ok that numbers didn't play your game,
rather they have lead you to mirk.
It's ok that you had to leave them alone or them, alone
or if you were to be that left out one, trust me hon!
It's ok because you exist among those whose zones
aren't the same as those before, once it's dawn.
It's ok to be cautious while walking over thin ice.
It's ok if the ice broke,
and it's even ok 'cause you woke.
It's ok if you forget to sleep and equally ok
if you don't remember to wake.
Sometimes, it's okay to be.
Just be.

It's okay.
Because, you never wanted to ask.
