Haunted Closet

Dec, 2019 | SantDas

Turbid trees never remembered to grow
In the midst of which smokes out thou.
Is it but your eerie shriek, I revere
Or the wrath of your wraith, they fear?
Whatever, I wish not to dig you up, dear
And you too shall not drench me in tear.
So don't you get to me, or to me mere!
