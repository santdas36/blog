Haunted Closet

Curtains worried the wind and the ow,
Turbid trees never remembered to grow
In the midst of which smokes out thou.
Is it but your eerie shriek, they revere
Or the wrath of your wraith, they fear?
Whatever, I wish not to dig you up, dear
And you too shall not drench me in tear.
So don't you get to me, or to me mere!

No novel was that day. The Sun went into hiding as always, and I was gearing up to hunt down my thoughts. But only till then. Such things were to happen on a diurnal cycle, especially during the days of extremity in coldness under the enigma of moon! But that day pitched on top of the crests of waves, which I suppose must have forgotten to accompanied the troughs. I could have opted to go in with my brother to his, but I thought nothing worse than him shall peep into my room and choke me to death.

So, yes, with a sense of snoopiness and a lot of courage, I curbed myself to one of the four corners, didn't know which, but the one that stayed right across the window, so that I can clearly espy on whatsoever was upon me, eagerly awaiting to reach me and make sure I stay at the farthest possible away from it! Wish they had legs - poor trees! I can even count their leaves, it was winter, dude! My old grannie's measuring tapes are bootless. My eyes have never seen them grow since June of 2001. I wasn't born before. Yeah. Good old memories flash up, let me save them for the next article XD.

And suddenly, somewhere between those trees in my backyard, arose a brouhaha of babbling tenor. My ears bugged out, I felt that in my veins, coursing through and then the push. It started to smoke out in the coldest of winter nights, soothing but striking! Might the curtains have been a layer of pellucid disturbance, they flew into the woods. Now, none between me and... Whaaattt!! I saw her, she... she was wearing a long white robe. A glimpse of disillusion and dismay, the adrenaline rush. Can I see you again? Before I could ask, the smoke and the warmth retraced back.

Morning. I'm done with that gorge' dream, I swear. With that being said, I'm awake. The snow was real and the heaters didn't work.

"Momma, where's ma jacket?"

"Why can't you take care of your things yourself, you useless?" (ignore :p)

"Check your closet!" she shouted from the downstairs.

My legs trembled (of cold), but still I managed to reach the closet, a few steps from my bed. I tried, but it won't open! I anointed the rusty orange door-hinge, with lubricants but in vain. No other way, I had to share my Touche's stinky sweater. I went out to the backyard, and they haven't grown a bit, they wished me good with their white blossoms. Later then, I went through my usual chores, the same tiring things I've been doing since the advent of the winter birds. Write, sleep, dream, repeat.
